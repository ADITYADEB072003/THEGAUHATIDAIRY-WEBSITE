A GUWAHATI INSTITUTION

Pure dairy, timeless taste.

For nearly a century, The Gauhati Dairy has brought families together over rich, honest sweets. From our signature Kalakand to warm evening snacks, every bite is made for sharing.

Established in 1928 in the heart of Pan Bazaar, our heritage is built on the foundation of purity and craftsmanship. We continue to use traditional recipes passed down through generations to ensure that the taste of Guwahati remains as authentic today as it was nine decades ago.
